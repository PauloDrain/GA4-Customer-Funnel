# GA4-Customer-Funnel
Where in the GA4 Customer Funnel are they losing customers and resolutions to increase it

Key Findings:
Customers were being lost in the first stages of the funnel, having only 4.93% of people who enter a site click an item
If shown a promotion at the start it increased the amount of people who went through the customer funnel
The second hurdle was at the end where only 59.2% of people submitted their payment information
Out of the total number of people who paid, 53% of people paid with a coupon as opposed to those who didn't
